NSDI '25 accepted **83 papers** across two sessions, and the program reads like a networking conference that has absorbed the weight of ML workloads without giving up its own agenda. LLM training and serving infrastructure is now a full track — 14 of 83 papers touch collectives, checkpointing, fault localization, or inference serving — and even the classical datacenter-transport track leans on deployment evidence from Alibaba Cloud CDN, ByteDance training clusters, Google Colossus, and Meta federated analytics. Two cross-cutting tensions organize the rest: *"programmable" now means full-stack* (SmartNICs, Tofino switches, RNICs, eBPF, service meshes all get compilers and verifiers at NSDI '25), and *runtime diagnosis has replaced offline analysis* as the preferred way to reason about distributed behavior, whether the target is an RDMA container fleet, a distributed protocol implementation, or a 12k-GPU training job.

## Themes

- **LLM and ML workloads reshape the networking agenda.** Training infra spans [SimAI](../papers/nsdi-2025/simai-unifying-architecture-design-and-performance-tuning-for-large-scale-large-language.md) unifying capacity planning with packet-level tuning at 98.1% alignment, [RLHFuse](../papers/nsdi-2025/optimizing-rlhf-training-for-large-language-models-with-stage-fusion.md) fusing RLHF stages for 3.7x throughput, [ByteCheckpoint](../papers/nsdi-2025/bytecheckpoint-a-unified-checkpointing-system-for-large-foundation-model-development.md) reshardable-at-load-time checkpoints, and [Multiverse](../papers/nsdi-2025/accelerating-design-space-exploration-for-llm-training-systems-with-multi-experiment.md) batching simulations as an ECS pipeline for 73x exploration speedup. Diagnosis has its own cluster: [Holmes](../papers/nsdi-2025/holmes-localizing-irregularities-in-llm-training-with-mega-scale-gpu-clusters.md), [Minder](../papers/nsdi-2025/minder-faulty-machine-detection-for-large-scale-distributed-model-training.md), and [Aegis](../papers/nsdi-2025/evolution-of-aegis-fault-diagnosis-for-ai-model-training-service-in-production.md) all localize irregular machines in seconds from CCL logs. Collectives are tuned online by [AutoCCL](../papers/nsdi-2025/autoccl-automated-collective-communication-tuning-for-accelerating-distributed-and.md) and [OptiReduce](../papers/nsdi-2025/optireduce-resilient-and-tail-optimal-allreduce-for-distributed-deep-learning-in-the-cloud.md), while [SuperServe](../papers/nsdi-2025/superserve-fine-grained-inference-serving-for-unpredictable-workloads.md), [Prism](../papers/nsdi-2025/gpu-disaggregated-serving-for-deep-learning-recommendation-models-at-scale.md), and [RegenHance](../papers/nsdi-2025/region-based-content-enhancement-for-ef-cient-video-analytics-at-the-edge.md) push inference, DLRM, and edge analytics into the same "fine-grained, slack-aware" design space.

- **Programmable dataplanes enter their full-stack era.** [Alkali](../papers/nsdi-2025/enabling-portable-and-high-performance-smartnic-programs-with-alkali.md) compiles single-threaded SmartNIC programs across heterogeneous NICs with automatic cuts and state placement; [CRAM](../papers/nsdi-2025/scaling-ip-lookup-to-large-databases-using-the-cram-lens.md) treats Tofino-2 as a joint TCAM+SRAM substrate and scales IP lookup far beyond pure-TCAM designs; [State-Compute Replication](../papers/nsdi-2025/state-compute-replication-parallelizing-high-speed-stateful-packet-processing.md) round-robins packets across cores and piggybacks history so stateful flows scale beyond one core; [ClubHeap](../papers/nsdi-2025/clubheap-a-high-speed-and-scalable-priority-queue-for-programmable-packet-scheduling.md), [PACKS](../papers/nsdi-2025/everything-matters-in-programmable-packet-scheduling.md), and [SCRR](../papers/nsdi-2025/self-clocked-round-robin-packet-scheduling.md) implement PIFO-grade priority scheduling on real hardware; [P4RTC](../papers/nsdi-2025/when-p4-meets-run-to-completion-architecture.md) ports P4 onto run-to-completion ASICs; [OrbitCache](../papers/nsdi-2025/pushing-the-limits-of-in-network-caching-for-key-value-stores.md) keeps hot KV pairs circulating as packets; and [CATO](../papers/nsdi-2025/cato-end-to-end-optimization-of-ml-based-traffic-analysis-pipelines.md) jointly searches features and packet depth for ML traffic analysis.

- **Far memory and granular resources become deployable substrate.** [Beehive](../papers/nsdi-2025/beehive-a-scalable-disaggregated-memory-runtime-exploiting-asynchrony-of-multithreaded.md) compiles remote accesses into lightweight pararoutines instead of many uthreads; [Eden](../papers/nsdi-2025/eden-developer-friendly-application-integrated-far-memory.md) handles most far-memory misses in user space with a few programmer hints; [ODRP](../papers/nsdi-2025/odrp-on-demand-remote-paging-with-programmable-rdma.md) turns chained RDMA WRs into a zero-CPU remote swap device with 4 KB page allocation; [Quicksand](../papers/nsdi-2025/quicksand-harnessing-stranded-datacenter-resources-with-granular-computing.md) and [GRANNY](../papers/nsdi-2025/granny-granular-management-of-compute-intensive-applications-in-the-cloud.md) harvest stranded CPU/RAM by keeping work small and migratable; and [Leopard](../papers/nsdi-2025/making-serverless-pay-for-use-a-reality-with-leopard.md) takes the final billing step by exposing spot/preemptible resources directly to serverless pricing.

- **Production deployment stories talk back.** A rare density of hyperscaler papers ships real measurements: Google [cuts ToR hotspots](../papers/nsdi-2025/preventing-network-bottlenecks-accelerating-datacenter-services-with-hotspot-aware.md) by 90% with hotspot-aware placement and [walks through QoS alignment with Aequitas](../papers/nsdi-2025/learnings-from-deploying-network-qos-alignment-to-application-priorities-for-storage.md) on storage RPCs; Alibaba Cloud reports on [Aegis training diagnosis](../papers/nsdi-2025/evolution-of-aegis-fault-diagnosis-for-ai-model-training-service-in-production.md), [CDN congestion-control selection](../papers/nsdi-2025/learning-production-optimized-congestion-control-selection-for-alibaba-cloud-cdn.md), and [RDMA container-network scalability](../papers/nsdi-2025/mitigating-scalability-walls-of-rdma-based-container-networks.md); ByteDance ships [ByteCheckpoint](../papers/nsdi-2025/bytecheckpoint-a-unified-checkpointing-system-for-large-foundation-model-development.md) and [Minder](../papers/nsdi-2025/minder-faulty-machine-detection-for-large-scale-distributed-model-training.md); Meta documents [PAPAYA](../papers/nsdi-2025/papaya-federated-analytics-stack-engineering-privacy-scalability-and-practicality.md) federated analytics at ~100M phones; and a cross-system study of [fail-slow tolerance](../papers/nsdi-2025/one-size-fits-none-understanding-and-enhancing-slow-fault-tolerance-in-modern-distributed.md) replaces brittle timeouts with adaptive detection.

- **Verification stretches across the stack.** [NDD](../papers/nsdi-2025/ndd-a-decision-diagram-for-network-verification.md) lifts BDD-based verification to the field level and removes atom-explosion bottlenecks; [Velo](../papers/nsdi-2025/verifying-maximum-link-loads-in-a-changing-world.md) verifies per-link worst-case loads under failures and BGP changes; [p4tv](../papers/nsdi-2025/on-temporal-verification-of-stateful-p4-programs.md) brings P4LTL temporal verification to stateful P4; [VEP](../papers/nsdi-2025/vep-a-two-stage-verification-toolchain-for-full-ebpf-programmability.md) moves eBPF proof search to user space with a tiny kernel checker; [QUASI](../papers/nsdi-2025/a-layered-formal-methods-approach-to-answering-queue-related-queries.md) answers queue-related queries with a sound abstraction layer over SMT; [MeshTest](../papers/nsdi-2025/meshtest-end-to-end-testing-for-service-mesh-traffic-management.md) generates connected service-mesh configurations and expected results; [Ellsberg](../papers/nsdi-2025/runtime-protocol-refinement-checking-for-distributed-protocol-implementations.md) refines distributed implementations against a protocol model at runtime; [Smart Casual](../papers/nsdi-2025/smart-casual-verification-of-the-confidential-consortium-framework.md) pairs TLA+ with CI trace validation for Microsoft's CCF; and [CEGS](../papers/nsdi-2025/cegs-configuration-example-generalizing-synthesizer.md) synthesizes configurations from vendor examples with GNN + LLM + SMT.

## Notable trends

- **Learn what to run, don't hand-tune it.** [Mutant](../papers/nsdi-2025/mutant-learning-congestion-control-from-existing-protocols-via-online-reinforcement.md), [ALI CCS](../papers/nsdi-2025/learning-production-optimized-congestion-control-selection-for-alibaba-cloud-cdn.md), and [Mowgli](../papers/nsdi-2025/mowgli-passively-learned-rate-control-for-real-time-video.md) all pick a rate-control policy online — via contextual bandit, production logs, or conservative offline RL on GCC telemetry — instead of committing to one CC at compile time. [BFTBrain](../papers/nsdi-2025/bftbrain-adaptive-bft-consensus-with-reinforcement-learning.md) does the same for Byzantine protocols; [AutoCCL](../papers/nsdi-2025/autoccl-automated-collective-communication-tuning-for-accelerating-distributed-and.md) and [CATO](../papers/nsdi-2025/cato-end-to-end-optimization-of-ml-based-traffic-analysis-pipelines.md) search tuning or feature knobs with Bayesian optimization. The shared move: treat the knob space as first-class and run the search online.

- **Transport gets decomposed: who schedules, who pauses, who feeds back.** [SIRD](../papers/nsdi-2025/sird-a-sender-informed-receiver-driven-datacenter-transport-protocol.md) keeps receiver-driven scheduling on exclusive downlinks but adds sender-informed credit rationing on shared links; [Pyrrha](../papers/nsdi-2025/pyrrha-congestion-root-based-flow-control-to-eliminate-head-of-line-blocking-in-datacenter.md) pauses only flows that feed each congestion root; [MTP](../papers/nsdi-2025/mtp-transport-for-in-network-computing.md) moves transport to messages and pathlets so in-network offloads can mutate traffic safely; [White-Boxing RDMA](../papers/nsdi-2025/white-boxing-rdma-with-packet-granular-software-control.md) turns BlueField-3 per-QP dequeue into a software control point; [eTran](../papers/nsdi-2025/etran-extensible-kernel-transport-with-ebpf.md) adds eBPF hooks so kernel transports approach user-space performance.

- **Runtime diagnosis replaces offline analysis.** [Ellsberg](../papers/nsdi-2025/runtime-protocol-refinement-checking-for-distributed-protocol-implementations.md) and [Smart Casual](../papers/nsdi-2025/smart-casual-verification-of-the-confidential-consortium-framework.md) refine deployed code against formal models from traces; [Holmes](../papers/nsdi-2025/holmes-localizing-irregularities-in-llm-training-with-mega-scale-gpu-clusters.md), [Minder](../papers/nsdi-2025/minder-faulty-machine-detection-for-large-scale-distributed-model-training.md), and [Aegis](../papers/nsdi-2025/evolution-of-aegis-fault-diagnosis-for-ai-model-training-service-in-production.md) localize irregular machines from CCL logs; [ntprof](../papers/nsdi-2025/understanding-and-profiling-nvme-over-tcp-using-ntprof.md) profiles NVMe/TCP as a chain of software switches; [ScalaCN](../papers/nsdi-2025/mitigating-scalability-walls-of-rdma-based-container-networks.md) uses combinatorial causal testing on live RDMA container networks; and [One-Size-Fits-None](../papers/nsdi-2025/one-size-fits-none-understanding-and-enhancing-slow-fault-tolerance-in-modern-distributed.md) replaces fixed timeouts with runtime-adaptive slow-fault detection.

- **Programmable switches finally do scheduling, priority, and caching, not just matching.** [ClubHeap](../papers/nsdi-2025/clubheap-a-high-speed-and-scalable-priority-queue-for-programmable-packet-scheduling.md) fully pipelines PIFO at one replace per cycle; [PACKS](../papers/nsdi-2025/everything-matters-in-programmable-packet-scheduling.md) pairs quantile admission with occupancy-aware queue mapping; [SCRR](../papers/nsdi-2025/self-clocked-round-robin-packet-scheduling.md) replaces DRR's fixed quantum with self-clocked virtual time; [OrbitCache](../papers/nsdi-2025/pushing-the-limits-of-in-network-caching-for-key-value-stores.md) caches variable-length KV items as packets; [CRAM](../papers/nsdi-2025/scaling-ip-lookup-to-large-databases-using-the-cram-lens.md) fits modern IP lookup tables inside Tofino-2; and [P4RTC](../papers/nsdi-2025/when-p4-meets-run-to-completion-architecture.md) makes run-to-completion ASICs a first-class P4 target.

## Must-read picks

- **[SimAI](../papers/nsdi-2025/simai-unifying-architecture-design-and-performance-tuning-for-large-scale-large-language.md)** — rare LLM-training simulator that unifies capacity planning and packet-level tuning at 98.1% alignment with real runs by reusing real training frameworks and NCCL. Directly usable for pre-deployment architecture decisions at scale.
- **[Beehive](../papers/nsdi-2025/beehive-a-scalable-disaggregated-memory-runtime-exploiting-asynchrony-of-multithreaded.md)** — pararoutines replace the "many uthreads + context switch" pattern for far memory, giving disaggregated-memory runtimes a programming model that stays synchronous at the source level.
- **[Ellsberg](../papers/nsdi-2025/runtime-protocol-refinement-checking-for-distributed-protocol-implementations.md)** — introduces runtime protocol refinement checking: deploy-as-is, reconstruct the protocol trace, and compare against a model without service changes or cross-node coordination.
- **[Aequitas](../papers/nsdi-2025/learnings-from-deploying-network-qos-alignment-to-application-priorities-for-storage.md)** — Google-scale production evidence that fixing RPC-to-DSCP/WFQ alignment lowers storage latency even when it downgrades some flows; the kind of deployment report NSDI is uniquely positioned to publish.
- **[Smart Casual Verification of CCF](../papers/nsdi-2025/smart-casual-verification-of-the-confidential-consortium-framework.md)** — pairs TLA+ with trace validation against CCF's C++ tests in CI, giving a real confidential-consortium service a reproducible path from spec to implementation. A template that other production services can follow.
